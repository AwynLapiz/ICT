Accountancy, Business and Management Track

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management): This strand is designed for students who are interested in pursuing careers in business, finance, and management. It includes subjects such as accounting, economics, entrepreneurship, marketing, and business law. It aims to develop students' skills in critical thinking, problem solving, communication, and decision-making as well as prepare them for further studies in business-related fields.

Humanities and Social Sciences Track

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences): This strand is focused on developing students' understanding of human behavior, society, and culture. It includes subjects such as history, economics, political science, sociology, psychology, and literature. It aims to prepare students for careers in fields such as law, journalism, social work, teaching, and politics.

Information and Communication Technology Track

ICT (Information and Communications Technology): This strand focuses on developing students' skills and knowledge in the field of technology, particularly in the areas of computer hardware and software, programming, networking, and multimedia. It aims to prepare students for careers in the tech industry and related fields.

Agri-Fishery Arts Track

AFA (Arts and Design): This strand is designed for students who are interested in pursuing careers in the arts, such as visual arts, music, dance, and theater. It aims to develop their creativity, artistic skills, and knowledge of different art forms, as well as prepare them for further studies in the arts.
